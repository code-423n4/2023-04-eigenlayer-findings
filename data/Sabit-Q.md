The "=" sign signifies a fixed version pragma syntax. This means that the contract will always be compiled
with that specific version of the compiler regardless of any potential bug fixes, security patches,
or improvements that a later version can have.

Furthermore, it prevents taking advantage of updates and bug fixes that may be present in newer versions.
It locks a contract into an older, potentially less secure version of Solidity compiler - exposing 
a smart contract to vulnerabilities.

It is better to use a version number you're sure is secure without being fixed (=) or flexible (^).
